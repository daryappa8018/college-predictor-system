Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the predictions?

Our predictions are based on historical data from 2016-2024. While highly accurate, actual cutoffs may vary.

What is the 5-step fallback search?

If no colleges match your criteria, our system automatically tries different combinations of round, quota, pool, and institute type to find the best matches.

Can I see predictions for previous years?

Yes, registered users can view their complete search history with all previous predictions.

What's the difference between guest and registered users?

Guests get top 3 predictions. Registered users get top 30 predictions, search history, and PDF/Excel export options.

How do I interpret the match score?

Match score indicates how well your rank fits within the college's rank range. 100% means your rank is better than the opening rank.
