About College Predictor System

Helping JEE aspirants find their perfect college match

Our Mission

We aim to simplify the college selection process for JEE aspirants by providing accurate, data-driven predictions based on historical admission data from 23 IITs and 31 NITs.

What We Offer

  • AI-powered college predictions
  • Smart 5-step fallback search system
  • Support for all quotas (AI, HS, OS, AP, GO, JK, LA)
  • Gender-Neutral and Female-Only pool options
    • Complete search history tracking
  • PDF & Excel export options

Data Coverage

64,958 admission records from years 2016-2024, covering:

  • 23 IITs + 31 NITs
  • 10 categories (GEN, OBC-NCL, SC, ST, EWS, PWD variants)
  • 7 quota types
  • 7 counseling rounds
